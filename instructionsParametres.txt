Please select the parameters:
	
	Population 	The size of the population.
	
	Infectives1	The rate of people infected with only disease 1.
	Infectives2	The rate of people infected with only disease 2.
	Infectives12	The rate of people infected with disease 1 and disease 2.
	Susceptibles	The rate of people not infected with any of the diseases. They are susceptibles to be infected with both.

	Male 		The rate of the male population.
	Female		The rate of the female population.

	Young		The rate of the young-age population (.....).
	Medium		The rate of the medium-age population (.....).
	Old		The rate of the old-age population (.....).
